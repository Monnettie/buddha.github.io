Donate:

Support these groups and learn more about Buddhism

Dharma Relief

Currently raising money for COVID-19 relief and racial equailty. Many major Buddhist organizations such as LionsRoar, Buddhadharma, Barre Institute for Buddhist Studies, Sravasti Abbey, and Dharma Drum are currently sponsoring it.

Buddhist Global Relief

Guided by Buddhist teachings, BGR seeks to to provide aide to stuggling communities around the world. They provide food, financial support, and education to those in need.

Dharma Drum Mountain

Has multiple campaigns and charities for different causes. These include environmental protection, community preservation, international relief, and so much more. They are also dedicated to teaching Buddhism and have many resources for the new and old.

Fo Guang Shan

Promotes Humanistic Buddhism and is known for its efforts in the modernization of Chinese Buddhism. The order is famous for its use of technology and its temples are often furnished with the latest equipment. They have many articles, books, and in person retreats for all to join.

Barre Institute for Buddhist Studies

Offers full course education for Buddhist teachings, both online and in person. To support beginner's they have crafted full courses to help guide them through their journey's
